1. Overview

JUTTFHX Sensitivity Hub ("we", "us") runs community Free Fire tournaments through this website. This policy explains what information we collect when you register for a tournament, how we use it, and — importantly — our policy on tournament entry fees. It applies in addition to any general data handling already described elsewhere on the site (e.g. account sign-in, analytics).

2. Information We Collect

When you register for a tournament, we ask for:

  • In-game UID and In-game name (IGN) — used to verify your identity inside Free Fire and add you to the correct lobby.
  • Player level — used for basic eligibility checks on some events.
  • WhatsApp / contact number — used to send you lobby codes, schedule changes, or to reach you if there's an issue with your entry.
  • Payment method and a screenshot of your payment (paid events only) — used solely to confirm your entry fee was paid before you're added to the bracket.

We do not ask for your Free Fire account password, OTPs, or any platform login credentials. We will never ask you for these — do not share them with anyone claiming to represent JUTTFHX.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm and process your tournament registration.
  • To verify payment for paid events before granting entry.
  • To contact you about lobby codes, scheduling, disputes, or disqualifications related to the event you registered for.
  • To enforce our daily free-tournament entry limit and prevent abuse (e.g. one player registering dozens of times under different names).
  • To maintain a basic record of past participants for leaderboard, prize distribution, and fraud-prevention purposes.

We do not sell your information, and we do not use tournament registration data for advertising.

4. Tournament Entry Fees & Refund Policy

All paid tournament entry fees are strictly non-refundable once submitted.

This means we will not issue a refund for reasons including, but not limited to:

  • Early elimination, a loss, or any in-match outcome.
  • Disqualification for breaking event rules (teaming, smurfing, hacking, no-shows, etc).
  • Connection issues, lag, crashes, or device problems on your end.
  • Submitting an incorrect UID, IGN, or payment screenshot.
  • Changing your mind after registering.

If we cancel or indefinitely postpone an event ourselves before it starts, entry fees for that specific event may be refunded or carried over to a future event at our discretion — this is the only circumstance under which a refund may be considered.

5. Payment Processing & Screenshots

We currently accept payments via Easypaisa, NayaPay, and direct bank transfer. These are third-party financial services — we do not process or store your card, bank login, or wallet credentials; you complete the transfer directly in your own banking/wallet app and then upload a screenshot to us as proof.

Payment screenshots are used only to verify that a transfer was made and matches the amount/details for the event you're registering for. We are not responsible for transaction failures, delays, or disputes caused by the payment provider itself (Easypaisa, NayaPay, or your bank) — those should be raised directly with that provider. Screenshots are retained only as long as needed to verify your entry and resolve any disputes about that event, after which they may be deleted.

6. Free Tournament Daily Limit

To keep free events fair for everyone, each user may register for a maximum of 2 free tournaments per day. This limit resets at midnight (local device time) and does not apply to paid events. We track this using a counter stored locally in your browser — it is not shared with third parties.

7. Data Retention & Your Rights

We keep tournament registration data (UID, IGN, level, contact, payment proof) for as long as reasonably needed to run the event, resolve disputes, and maintain basic records — typically no longer than a few months after an event concludes, unless required for an ongoing dispute. You can request that we delete your tournament registration data, or ask what we hold about you, by emailing us at the address below.

8. Cookies & Analytics

Like most sites, we use basic analytics (Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity) to understand how the site is used. This is separate from tournament registration data and is not used to identify you personally for tournament purposes.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our tournament features change. Significant changes (e.g. to the refund policy) will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy, a tournament entry, or a payment issue? Email us at contact@juttfhx.app.
